What?

Welcome to Dulceta. In this site, you'll be able to view all my different art pieces and sketches collected from the past few years.

Art is an incredibly important aspect of my life, as is best labeled as an outlet for me. I find myself drawing or painting when I am stressed or anxious because the overall process is therapeutic. Not only this, but art opens doors for expression and self reflection. Rather than envoking emotion or a thought via words, art produces the same result through images and appearance.

Ever since I was a little girl, a pencil has always been in my hand. I encourage anyone and everyone to find an outlet whether it is drawing, writing, playing an instrument, etc. I can gurantee that finding a passion or simply a hobby will be one of the best things to happen to you.


If you want to look through some other people's works or you want inspiration, check out Colossal.
