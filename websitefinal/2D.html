The Importance of 2D

I happen to enjoy working with traditional mediums, isnce I have more experience and knowledge. While this may be a little tougher compared to digital (as there is no easy way to undo a mistake) I still enjoy it just as much. The first medium I ever used was watercolor in preschool and onto oil paints in kindergarten.

I find it incredibly helpful to have a strong technical background in drawing and sketching as it is very applicale and useful to many things. You can see in the pieces above that I dabble in multiple mediums such as acrylic, watercolor, and even colored pencil.

Tips!

My only advice here is to practice as much as possible and play around with all sorts of mediums.
